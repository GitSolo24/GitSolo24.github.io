my background

I am originally a mechanical engineer and I have worked in the manufacturing and
construction industries. I also got the opportunity to get involved in
project management. Now I am also in the Technology space

I started my engineering journey back in 2010, studying and obtaining a Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering in 2015. I also had internship opportunities at an energy company. I further proceeded to obtain a Masters degree in Mechanical engineering as well. I entered the workforce as a manufacturing engineer in the automotive industry but things do not always go as planned. My next stop was as a project engineer. I built small application to manage building equipment inventory and also managed a cloud model. I worked with the technology team to implement various forms of technology in the construction process. These experiences, coupled with multiple programming projects while studying engineering influenced me to return to school for technology. I earned my Masters in Computer Information Systems from Prairie View A&M University in 2022 and I am looking for my next adventure in the technology space
